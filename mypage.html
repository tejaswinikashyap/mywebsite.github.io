Construction-Turnkey Projects

A turnkey project is a type of project that it is ready for immediate use.The word is a reference to the fact that the customer,upon receiving the product,just needs to turn the igniyion key to make it operational,or that the key just needs to be turned over to the customer.If SAC builds a "turnkey home" we frame the structure and finish the interior.Everything is completed down to the cabinets and carpet."Turnkey"is commonly used in the construction industry,for instance,in which it refers to the bundling of materials and labor.
